Frequently Asked Questions

What is PocketChain?

PocketChain is a professional decentralized finance (DeFi) protocol built on blockchain technology. It offers staking services, crypto wallet management, and yield generation opportunities with institutional-grade security.

How does PCH Wallet work?

PCH Wallet is a non-custodial wallet, meaning your private keys and passphrase are exclusively accessible only to you. You have full control over your assets. You can unlock your wallet using your passphrase or biometric authentication (fingerprint).

What is staking and how do I earn rewards?

Staking is the process of locking your PCH tokens to support the network and earn rewards. Choose from our flexible staking pools with APY rates ranging from 15% to 125% depending on the lock period. Rewards are calculated daily and distributed at the end of the staking period.

How do I deposit crypto to my PCH Wallet?

To deposit crypto, go to your PCH Wallet and click the "Deposit" button. You can transfer crypto from any on-chain wallet or exchange by sending it to your unique PCH wallet address. Always verify the address before sending.

What are the withdrawal options?

We offer two withdrawal methods: P2P (Peer-to-Peer) for buying/selling crypto with PHP, and direct crypto withdrawal to external wallets or exchanges. Network fees of 0.5 PCH apply for crypto withdrawals.

Is KYC verification required?

KYC verification is required to unlock full platform features and higher withdrawal limits. The verification process typically takes 24-48 hours. Complete your KYC in the Account section under "My Account."

How secure is my wallet?

Your PCH Wallet uses industry-leading security measures including encryption, multi-signature support, and biometric authentication. As a non-custodial wallet, you maintain full control of your private keys. Never share your passphrase with anyone.

How can I earn through the Airdrop program?

Participate in our Airdrop program by completing daily attendance check-ins, inviting friends using your referral code, and completing social media tasks. Earn up to 30 PCH tokens through social tasks and 5 PCH for each successful referral.
